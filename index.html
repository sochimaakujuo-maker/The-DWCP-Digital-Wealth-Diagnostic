What Is Actually Going On

You tried something. Maybe graphic design. Maybe content creation. Maybe you paid for a course, did half of it, and stopped. Maybe you announced something in January and had quietly dropped it by March.

Earning from the right skill is not about any one thing. It is about five things working together. When they don't, you feel it everywhere: in your answer when someone asks "so what are you doing?", in how you price your work, in the quiet feeling that you are capable of more than you are currently earning.
